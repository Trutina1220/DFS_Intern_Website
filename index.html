Intern Placement:
Business Management Students

Placements emphasize on students' learning and growth. Through DFS' Business Integration Initiative, DFS works closely with partnered institutions to have students experience working ini a real business based in Australia. Each student has a small role within a real project and should gain an understanding about how our business is run by the end of the 12-week period.
