Emergency Contact Information

  • Emergency Services
    • Phone: 000
    • Service: For immediate emergency assistance from police, fire, or ambulance services.
  • Lifeline
    • Phone: 13 11 14
    • Website: Lifeline Australia
    • Service: Provides 24/7 crisis support and suicide prevention services.
  • Beyond Blue
    • Phone: 1300 22 4636
    • Website: Beyond Blue
    • Service: Offers support for anxiety, depression, and mental health issues, available 24/7.
  • Kids Helpline
    • Phone: 1800 55 1800
    • Website: Kids Helpline
    • Service: Free, 24/7 support for young people aged 5-25, including online chat and email support.
  • Suicide Call Back Service
    • Phone: 1300 659 467
    • Website: Suicide Call Back Service
    • Service: Provides 24/7 nationwide professional telephone and online counseling for anyone affected by suicide.
  • MensLine Australia
    • Phone: 1300 78 99 78
    • Website: MensLine Australia
    • Service: 24/7 telephone and online support for men with family and relationship concerns.
  • 1800RESPECT
    • Phone: 1800 737 732
    • Website: 1800RESPECT
    • Service: National sexual assault, domestic and family violence counselling service, available 24/7.
  • SANE Australia
    • Phone: 1800 187 263
    • Website: SANE Australia
    • Service: Provides support for people with complex mental health issues and their families.
  • National Alcohol and Drug Hotline NSW
    • Phone: 1800 250 015
    • Website: NSW Health
    • Service: Offers confidential advice and support for people with alcohol and drug problems.
  • Q Life
    • Phone: 1800 184 527
    • Website: Q Life
    • Service: Provides confidential counselling and support for LGBTIQ+ people.
  • Griefline
    • Phone: 1300 845 745
    • Website: Griefline
    • Service: Provides support and information for people experiencing grief and loss.
  • National Debt Helpline
    • Phone: 1800 007 007
    • Website: National Debt Helpline
    • Service: Offers free financial advice and support for people struggling with debt.
